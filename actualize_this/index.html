the strangest yellow

I always lock the door when I creep by daylight.

“I cry at nothing, and cry most of the time.”

The color is hideous enough, and unreliable enough, and infuriating enough, but the pattern is torturing. It is the strangest yellow, that wallpaper! It makes me think of all the yellow things I ever saw - not beautiful ones like buttercups, but old foul, bad yellow things.

You see I have something more to expect, to look forward to, to watch. I really do eat better, and am more quiet than I was.

Sometimes I think there are a great many women behind,
and sometimes only one, and she crawls around fast, and her crawling shakes it all over.
But nobody could climb through that pattern--it strangles so;
I think that is why it has so many heads.
